Our Story

A beacon on Boston Harbor

Founded in 1964, UMass Boston is Massachusetts' only public research university in Boston. We are an anchor institution for the city — committed to excellence in teaching, research, and community engagement.

  • Carnegie R1 Doctoral University — Highest Research Activity designation
  • Located on a stunning 175-acre peninsula surrounded by Boston Harbor
  • Home to 10 colleges and 200+ undergraduate and graduate programs
  • Ranked among top universities for diversity and social mobility
  • $100M+ in annual sponsored research expenditures
