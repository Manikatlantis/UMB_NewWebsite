Our Story

A beacon on Boston Harbor

Founded in 1964, UMass Boston is the only public research university in the City of Boston — an anchor institution serving the Commonwealth with world-class education and scholarship.

  • Carnegie R1 classification — highest doctoral research activity
  • Located on a 175-acre Columbia Point peninsula, surrounded by harbor
  • 10 colleges spanning sciences, arts, business, health & policy
  • $100M+ in annual sponsored research funding
  • Adjacent to the JFK Presidential Library & Museum
